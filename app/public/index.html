About Me

As a full-stack developer, I started my career with ruby and rapidly improved my stack with JS (node/React). I have a background in managing best-in-class end-to-end projects, events and teams from different sizes and cultures, as well as budgeting and writing business plans.

Due to my background in hospitality and personality, I can communicate easily with clients and colleagues regardless of their level or needs. During the pandemic, I gave coffee and cocktails workshops to 800+ people.

I have recently been responsible for Google's internal catering services and external events in Amsterdam. After living in the Netherlands for the past four years, I've decided to pursue my love for technology by completing Le Wagon's Full-Stack Bootcamp.

Below the surface:

  • I usually cycle or code and host friends for board games and food in my free time.
  • I’m a former Bartender and Barista.

Ask me about: Ruby, JS, bikes, food, coffee and tech.

You can find my latest public projects on my GitHub or website as I keep working to improve myself and my work.
